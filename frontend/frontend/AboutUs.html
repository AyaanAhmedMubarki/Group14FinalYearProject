About MediConnectX

A smart, role-based medical management platform built to connect patients, doctors, and administrators — making healthcare operations structured, transparent, and efficient.

What is MediConnectX?

MediConnectX is an innovation project designed to bring order and clarity to everyday healthcare operations. Rather than juggling disconnected tools, MediConnectX unifies patient care, doctor responsiveness, and administrative control into one secure platform — accessible to everyone with the right role and the right purpose.

🏥

Health Event Management

Administrators create and manage health & wellness events. Patients discover and register for them. Admins approve or reject registrations — keeping every event organised and accountable.

🩺

Medical Assistance Requests

Patients can request direct medical assistance from a verified doctor of their choice. Doctors review each request and respond with a decision and personalised remarks.

📊

Results & Health Scores

After each health event, administrators publish participation results and scores for patients. Patients can view their performance history from their personal dashboard at any time.

👤

Profile Management

Every user — patient, doctor, or admin — maintains a rich profile with photo upload support. Doctors can showcase their specialisation; patients keep their health details current.

🔐

Secure Role-Based Access

Powered by JWT authentication and Spring Security, MediConnectX ensures every user only sees and does exactly what their role permits — no more, no less.

📋

Registration Approvals

No event registration goes unreviewed. Admins approve or reject patient sign-ups, giving the platform the oversight needed to run safe, well-managed health programmes.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To reduce friction in healthcare management by connecting patients, doctors, and administrators through a single, structured platform — where every request is trackable, every event is manageable, and every result is transparent.

🔭 Our Vision

A future where healthcare feels as simple as using an app — where patients are empowered, doctors are reachable, and administrators have full visibility over the operations they manage, all secured and accessible from anywhere.

Who Uses MediConnectX?

The platform is built around three distinct roles, each with a tailored experience and purpose-built tools.

🛡️

Administrators

  • Create and manage health events
  • Approve or reject patient registrations
  • Publish event results and scores
  • Oversee the entire platform
👨‍⚕️

Doctors

  • Maintain a professional profile
  • View upcoming health events
  • Review patient assistance requests
  • Respond with decisions and remarks
🏃

Patients

  • Browse and register for events
  • Track registration status
  • Request medical assistance from doctors
  • View published health results
