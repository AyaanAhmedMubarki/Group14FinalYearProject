About MediConnectX

A smart, role-based platform unifying patient care, doctor responsiveness, and administrative control into one secure, efficient ecosystem.

🛡️

Administrators

  • Create and manage health events
  • Approve patient registrations
  • Enforce secure role-based access
👨‍⚕️

Doctors

  • Maintain verified specialist profiles
  • Review assistance requests
  • Provide personalised medical remarks
🏃

Patients

  • Discover and register for events
  • Track registration approvals
  • Request direct doctor assistance
